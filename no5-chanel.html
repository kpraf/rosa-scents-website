About N°5

The now and forever fragrance. The ultimate in femininity. An elegant, luxurious spray closest in strength and character to the parfum form. The sleek, portable signature bottle is perfect for the dressing table or travel.

A rich, robust floral-aldehydic fragrance. An extravagant, full-bodied bouquet of abstract flowers with aldehydic sparkle. Powerful, yet restrained. Complex, yet measured. N°5 has become an undisputed icon of modern perfumery.
